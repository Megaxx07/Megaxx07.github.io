Performance Analysis of Six Trading Strategies in the Chip Industry
#Project 2021-11-01

Advisor: Adjunct Professor Sebastien Donadio (NYU Tandon FRE)
11/2021 – 12/2021
• Collected and cleaned financial and trading data of six listed chip companies such as AAPL, GOOG, and NVDA in 5 years using
Python and APIs (Yahoo, Capital IQ).
• Built simple trading strategies trying to use six machine learning algorithms such as bagging, neural network, SVM to find the
best three strategies. Collected daily frequency data and generated labels.
• Analyzed the performance of the best three trading strategies through win rate, PNL, Sharpe ratio, and annual return.
• The best three strategies all have Sharpe ratio over 1.0 and annual return over 17%
